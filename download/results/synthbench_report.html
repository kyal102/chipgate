Limitations

Important: ChipSynthBench uses RTL-level proxy metrics. It does not prove real silicon performance, real power consumption, timing closure, area after synthesis, fabrication readiness or physical safety. These proxy scores are based on RTL text heuristics (assignment count, expression nesting depth, signal toggle patterns). Real results require EDA synthesis, static timing analysis, and power estimation tools.
