Limitation: These are estimated verification-cost units under the synthetic benchmark cost model. They do not represent real-world EDA cost, GPU time, or monetary cost.
DTL-ChipBench is a model-free benchmark that tests the ChipGate/DTL verification gate itself. It uses deterministic synthetic RTL proposals and mutation-generated cases to measure whether unsafe or regressive chip-design changes are blocked before heavier verification is needed. It does not prove chip correctness, silicon readiness, physical safety, or that any AI model is faster, safer, or better at chip design. All cost figures are abstract benchmark cost units.
Model-Free Benchmark: These results are not model-connected yet. They measure ChipGate's deterministic gate behaviour on synthetic RTL and mutation-generated benchmark cases. They do not prove that any AI model is faster, safer or better at chip design.
