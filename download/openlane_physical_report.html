Public Disclaimer: OpenLanePhysicalBench checks whether a tiny DTL gate design can move from open-silicon preparation toward a reproducible ASIC physical-flow readiness review. It does not prove silicon correctness, fabrication readiness, timing closure, real power, real area, physical durability, regulatory compliance or safety-critical deployment.
Limitation: Passing OpenLanePhysicalBench does not mean the design has been fabricated, accepted by Tiny Tapeout, timing-closed, power-characterised, physically tested, certified, or proven safe for real-world actuation. It means the generated artifacts passed structural and fixture-based report-parsing checks. Actual physical results require official OpenLane flow, foundry PDK, DRC/LVS signoff, and physical measurement.
